Tony Stark

The man who saved billions of lives

Here are some story moments about this hero

  • SUIT UP - In a cave in Afghanistan, Tony Stark and Ho Yinsen developed the first Iron Man suit, which Stark used to escape his captors.
  • SAVING NEW YORK - Fighting alongside the Avengers for the first time, Iron Man nearly sacrificed himself in order to save New York.
  • NEAR DEATH - Stark was nearly killed when an attack by the supposed Mandarin destroyed his home, sending him plummeting into the sea.
  • SECRETS FROM THE PAST - Already on opposite sides of a fight over the Sokovia Accords, Iron Man unleashed his full fury on Captain America and Bucky when he learned the truth about his parents’ death.
  • FACING THE MAD TITAN - On the planet Titan, Iron Man, alongside several allies, took on Thanos in a bid to stop the Titan from gaining all of the Infinity Stones.

Here are some powers and abilities

  • FLIGHT - Iron Man’s suit enables Tony Stark to fly, with upgrades increasing the speed capabilities.
  • ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE - An onboard AI system – originally JARVIS, then replaced by FRIDAY – helps Tony control his armor and connect to other systems.
  • ARMORED SUIT - Tony has found increasingly advanced ways to quickly don the armor, from the capability to summon it to him, to nanotech that allows the armor to grow from within a storage unit on his chest.
  • SUPER STRENGTH + DURABILITY - One of many specialized suits, the Hulkbuster armor is a highly durable, incredibly strong suit that allows Stark to go toe to toe with the teammate for which it is named.
